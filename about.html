My Journey

Hi, I'm Ayush Patil — a tech enthusiast, lifelong learner, and developer passionate about building meaningful digital solutions.

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at Dr. D. Y. Patil Institute of Technology, Pune. My journey started with a deep curiosity for how computers work and evolved into a dedicated pursuit of software development, AI, and mobile app creation.

I enjoy working with:

Flutter for building cross-platform mobile apps
Python for scripting, backend development, and machine learning
AI & Machine Learning to explore data-driven intelligence and smart automation
Data Structures & Algorithms to refine logic and performance

I'm especially passionate about AI-powered automation, ethical innovation, and creating tech that truly impacts lives.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new tech trends, experimenting with side projects, or collaborating on open-source contributions.

Let's connect and create something impactful together!
